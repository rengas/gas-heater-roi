A City Energy salesperson knocked on my door asking if I wanted to convert my electric water heater to gas. I had no idea if the numbers actually worked out. So I built a calculator to find out.

Then I built one for aircon. Then refrigerators. Now there's a small site of payback calculators specifically tuned to Singapore — SP Group tariffs, NEA Energy Labelling Scheme data, GST, Climate Voucher.

## What's here

- **[Water Heater ROI](/water-heater/)** — gas vs. electric, with payback period and historical tariff charts.
- **[Aircon ROI](/aircon/)** — compare star ratings and system configurations, side-by-side.
- **[Refrigerator ROI](/refrigerator/)** — when does upgrading actually pay back?

Each calculator has its own methodology page exposing every formula. No black boxes.

## What's coming on this blog

Short, honest notes:

- Tariff-watch when SP or City Energy revises their rates.
- Deep dives on the math behind specific decisions (e.g. *why an electric storage tank quietly costs you $8/month even when you don't use it*).
- SG-specific rebate explainers — Climate Voucher, CDC schemes, NEA grants.
- Real numbers from actual households who try the calculators.
